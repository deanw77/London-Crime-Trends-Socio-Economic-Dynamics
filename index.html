London Crime Trends & Socio-Economic Dynamics

A deep dive into correlation between crime rates and house prices in the various boroughs of London

Welcome to London Crime Trends

This project explores the relationship between crime rates and socio-economic factors in London. It aims to provide insights into how various socio-economic dynamics influence crime trends across different boroughsand the effect that has on house prices

Project Overview

This project utilizes data from the UK Government's Open Data portal, focusing on crime statistics and socio-economic indicators in London. The analysis includes:

  • Crime rates by borough
  • Socio-economic factors such as income, education, and employment
  • Correlation between crime rates and house prices

Data Sources

The data used in this project is sourced from:

  • London Crime Data
  • Borough Profiles
  • House Prices in London
