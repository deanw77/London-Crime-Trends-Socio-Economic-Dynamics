London Crime Trends


We first a brief look at the distribution of crime across the London Boroughs

We can see that central London is a hotspot for crime. This largely due to much higher rates of theft in central London. In fact theft Accounts for over 50% of all crime in Central London. The next most common crime is violence against the person, which accounts for around 20% of all crime in Central London.


If we filter by Burglary and Voilence we see a much more even distribution acfcross London Boroughs
